Where Vision Meets Reality

Rudram Design Studio is a contemporary architecture and interior design practice rooted in Lucknow, with projects spanning across India—from Kolkata to Rajasthan and beyond. Founded by Ar. Rudraakshi Tiwari, our studio brings together elite professional experience from New Delhi and Ahmedabad with a fresh, innovative approach to spatial design.

We believe that great design begins with listening. Our young, creative team is built on a foundation of patience, transparency, and an unwavering commitment to understanding your unique vision. We don't just design spaces—we craft experiences that seamlessly blend functionality with striking visual appeal.

Whether it's a contemporary residence, a bespoke commercial interior, or a complete home transformation, we deliver premium design aesthetics at competitive pricing, making sophisticated architecture accessible without compromise.
